# WinqLab Hero Refinement Design

**Date:** 2026-03-15
**Status:** Approved

## Goal

Refine the homepage hero so it feels calmer, more legible, and more aligned with `WinqLab` as a research-to-trading brand.

## User Feedback

1. The current headline is too crowded and uses punctuation that makes it feel heavier.
2. The line-height in the Chinese headline is too tight.
3. The right-side English-heavy interface panel is visually noisy.
4. The bottom English summary cards under the hero copy are distracting.
5. The hero should explicitly connect research with live trading.

## Approved Direction

Use a left-heavy hero with one cleaner supporting system panel on the right.

### Copy Direction

Replace the current headline:

`量化研究，从假设到系统化验证。`

with:

`从研究假设到系统验证，再到实盘交易`

Supporting copy should continue to emphasize structured research, but now acknowledge the path into live trading without sounding promotional.

### Layout Direction

1. Remove the top inline status pills in the hero copy.
2. Remove the bottom hero ledger cards under the CTA buttons.
3. Keep a right-side panel, but reduce it to one compact structure card.
4. The right panel should avoid dense English module lists and terminal-like traces.
5. Increase headline line-height and rebalance wrapping to avoid a cramped final line.

### Tone

The hero should still feel product-like and system-oriented, but quieter and more mature than the current "interface demo" feel.
