# WinqLab Hero Alignment Polish Design

**Date:** 2026-03-15
**Status:** Approved

## Goal

Refine the homepage hero spacing so the first screen feels tighter and the left and right columns feel more intentionally aligned.

## User Feedback

1. The hero's first section still has more top and bottom spacing than necessary.
2. The left and right sides do not yet feel aligned.
3. The right-side card should move upward slightly.
4. The preferred visual anchor is closer to the left-side body copy rather than floating too low.

## Approved Direction

Make a light-touch spacing pass without changing the overall hero composition.

### Spacing Direction

1. Reduce top padding above the hero.
2. Reduce bottom padding below the hero before the next section begins.
3. Keep the layout airy, but remove unnecessary empty space.

### Alignment Direction

1. Keep the existing two-column layout.
2. Move the right-side card upward so it feels better locked to the left column.
3. Bring the two columns into a more cohesive visual band without making the card dominate.

### Scope

Only tune spacing, offsets, and alignment. Do not change hero copy structure, remove sections, or redesign the card.
